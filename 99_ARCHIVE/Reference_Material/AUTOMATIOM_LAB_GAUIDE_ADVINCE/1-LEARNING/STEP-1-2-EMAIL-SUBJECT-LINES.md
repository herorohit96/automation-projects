## BONUS: IF YOU'RE GETTING LOW OPEN RATES

**Check these:**
1. Is your list engaged? (old list = low open)
2. Are you emailing at right time? (8-10 AM, 6-8 PM best)
3. Is your "from" name trusted? (personal name > company name)
4. Are you in spam folder? (check deliverability)

**Fix:**
- Re-engage old list (send "Still interested?" email)
- Change sending time (test 9 AM vs 6 PM)
- Use your real name instead of brand
- Add authentication (SPF, DKIM records)
