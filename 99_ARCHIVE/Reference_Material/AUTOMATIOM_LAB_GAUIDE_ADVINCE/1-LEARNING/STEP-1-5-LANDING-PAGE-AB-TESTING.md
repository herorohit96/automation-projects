## DESIGN ELEMENTS

### Color Psychology

**Primary CTA Button:**
- Color: Orange or Red
- Why: Creates urgency, high contrast
- Test: Also try Green (trust signal)

**Secondary Elements:**
- Trust badges: Green checkmarks
- Testimonials: Dark gray quotes
- Social proof: Gold stars

### Font + Layout

**Headline:**
- Size: 48px (desktop), 28px (mobile)
- Weight: Bold (700)
- Color: Dark blue or black

**Body Text:**
- Size: 16px
- Line height: 1.6 (breathing room)
- Color: Dark gray (#333)

**CTA Button:**
- Size: 18px font, 16px padding
- Width: 100% on mobile, auto on desktop
- Hover effect: Darker shade

### Images/Videos

**Option 1: Show Dashboard**
- Screenshot of earnings dashboard
- â‚¹50K highlighted in green
- Date range: "Month 1"

**Option 2: Your Face**
- You pointing at screen
- Genuine smile, casual clothing
- Builds trust

**Option 3: Before/After**
- Left: Manual spreadsheet (overwhelmed)
- Right: Automated dashboard (relaxed)

**Option 4: Community**
- Photo grid of customers
- Their testimonials
- Builds FOMO

**Recommendation: Use Option 1 + Option 2 (dashboard + your face)**
