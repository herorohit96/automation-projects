# Accounts and Tools

Yeh woh free tools hain jo aapko automation ke liye zaroori hain. Har tool ka business logic samjho, kyunki yeh aapke earning ka foundation banega.

## Required Tools

### n8n
- Yeh core automation tool hai
- Free, self-hosted ya cloud mein available
- Business logic: Yeh workflows banata hai jo repetitive tasks ko automate karta hai, jisse aap time save karte ho aur clients ko value dete ho

### Google Account
- Gmail, Google Sheets, Google Drive
- Business logic: Data storage aur communication ke liye. Most Indian businesses yeh use karte hain, toh integration easy hota hai

### Telegram
- Messaging app with bots
- Business logic: Indian market mein WhatsApp ke alternative ke roop mein. Notifications, alerts, aur customer communication ke liye perfect

### Email Service (Gmail ya custom)
- Professional communication
- Business logic: Client communication aur automated emails ke liye. Lead generation aur follow-ups mein kaam aata hai

### Google Drive
- File storage aur sharing
- Business logic: Templates, documents, aur data backup ke liye. Collaboration aur scalability ke liye zaroori

Har tool ko setup karo aur test karo. Yeh aapke toolkit ka part banenge, jisse aap professional automations bana sakenge.
