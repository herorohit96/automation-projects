# MOBILE / Content - Monument of Content Creation Assets

**Monument Type:** Content Production Hub  
**Purpose:** Centralize all content creation materials for monetization.  
**Monetization Potential:** Direct revenue from faceless content, courses, services.

## Sub-Monuments
- **Faceless_Content:** Scripts, outlines, assets for video/audio content
- **PDF_Courses:** Structured course materials and outlines
- **Automation_Services:** Service offerings and templates
- **Education_Products:** Educational content and products

## Preservation Rules
- Organize by content type and monetization potential
- Include production notes and decisions
- Track performance and learnings
- Version content iterations
